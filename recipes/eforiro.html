Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, red bell pepper, habanero pepper and half of the onion together using a food processor.
  2. If using fresh spinach, chop it up, wash and squeeze, and leave in a colander. If using frozen spinach, microwave for a few minutes.
  3. Heat palm oil in a pot on medium heat, add in your onions and stir for about 2 minutes.
  4. Add in your blended tomato and pepper mix and stir for about a minute.
  5. Add the bouillon powder, crayfish, black pepper, cayenne pepper and salt, and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Add the goat meat, mushroom, and any other meat you desire and let boil for another 5 minutes.
